Erin & Sophie— 6 months!

Hello, my love!! This is your second website gift for our 6 months!! I don't know if I'm supposed to not celebrate anniversaries during a break, but idk I just want to let myself believe you're celebrating too somewhere. I've never properly sent this to you since I rewrote it so here. 

I've always believed in true love. Magical moments in a forest. Bumps in the hallways and books falling. All the cliche fairytale Disney stuff. Then I grew up. I saw my own family divorcing, fighting, leaving, breaking up. I started to convince myself that I wasn't, ya know, pretty enough to be loved like that.
 Then I met you. You loved me like that. You made me feel like the prettiest girl in the world and you adored me and my body. Which was REALLY hard to believe haha. That's when I realized. 
The best love doesn't come from cliche Disney moments, it comes from th moments in between the lines of our stories that brought us together. The moments no author would give much credit to. The late night talks on Instagram, the study groups, the random conversations about anything and everything at the same time. 
I was used to being the type to catch feelings on the spot and shoving them down because it was "delusional" and "desperate". From the first study group when we were hanging out, I felt that twing in my heart if I'm being honest. Eliana saw the look in my eyes. And I tried my best to ignore it. I still wasn't over the guy who treated me like trash but you showed me what real love felt like and that's how I fell. I love you so, so much Erin. Looking at you is like looking into my future. 
Smiling like idiots, laughing, and spinning around the kitchen with music on a record player. Late rainy night talks in traffic.
 I'll always wait. I don't know how I'll make it work, but I will. I have to make it work. I can't love anyone else, I don't want to love anyone else, I only want you, Erin. I want to wake up and look up to see your deep ocean eyes staring back into mine. 
I'll wait forever if I have to. Like Gatsby, like Penelope, like Burr (😭), I'll wait. I'll better myself. I'll become a better version of myself so you can be loved properly without me constantly hurting you. I want us to be healthy. I want us to be real. Not just some fantasy I keep putting in my head.
I love you, Erin. So, so much. Always and forever. Happy 6 months<3 xoxo

So take your time exploring this little website I made!!<3
