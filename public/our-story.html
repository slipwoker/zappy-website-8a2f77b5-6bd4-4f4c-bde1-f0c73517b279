A Little Letter for Month Six

This page is a little love letter—just for you, Erin. A place to look back on our first six months together, filled with all the moments that made usus.

December 2024 – June 2025

Six months of laughter, late-night talks, and falling more in love with you every single day. Here's to many more adventures together. 💕

Dear Erin,

Six months ago, I didn't know that someone could make my world feel so much brighter just by being in it. You walked into my life with your smile, your kindness, and that way you have of making everything feel like an adventure—even the quiet moments.

You bring out a version of me I didn't know existed. With you, I'm braver, sillier, more honest. You've taught me that love isn't just butterflies and grand gestures—it's also the way you remember how I like my coffee, the way you laugh at my terrible jokes, and how you hold my hand when the world feels too big.

I still think about our first real date—how nervous I was, how you made me feel so at ease. That night, sitting under the stars with you, I knew something had shifted. I knew I wanted more nights like that. More laughter. More of you.

These six months have been filled with little moments that have become my favorite memories. Late-night conversations that stretched into sunrise. Dancing in the kitchen. The way you look at me when you think I'm not watching. Every day with you feels like a gift I didn't know I was waiting for.

I don't know what the future holds, but I know I want to keep building it with you. Here's to six months of us—and to so many more moments, memories, and adventures together. Thank you for choosing me, for seeing me, for loving me exactly as I am.

— Sophie

Our little milestones

6 months

Half a year of laughter, late-night talks, and discovering new favorite things together. Time flies when you're building something beautiful.

First tradition

Sunday morning pancakes and playlist swaps. It's the little rituals that make us feel like home, no matter where we are.

First trip

That weekend getaway where we got lost twice, found the best coffee shop, and laughed until our sides hurt. Adventure looks good on us.

Favorite shared comfort

Cozy blankets, Studio Ghibli movies, and your head on my shoulder. Sometimes the best moments are the quiet ones where we just exist together.

Funniest moment

When you tried to surprise me with breakfast in bed but tripped on the rug and nearly launched pancakes into orbit. We laughed for twenty minutes straight.

Best lesson so far

Love isn't just the big gestures—it's remembering how someone takes their coffee, texting them silly memes, and choosing each other every single day.
