A few gentle prompts

If you're not sure what to write, here are some sweet questions to spark your thoughts. Take your time, and write from the heart—there's no rush, no right answer. Just you and your love for Sophie.

What's a small moment from this month that made you smile?

Think of something tiny—a shared glance, a silly joke, a quiet morning together. The little things often mean the most.

Is there a tradition (big or small) you'd like to keep?

Maybe it's Sunday pancakes, late-night talks, or the way you say goodnight. What ritual feels like "us" that you want to protect?

What's a compliment you want Sophie to remember forever?

Something true and tender. What quality, habit, or part of who she is do you admire most? Let her know.
